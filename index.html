About AIMTDS

African Institute of Management, Technology and Development Studies(AIMTDS) is a leading educational, research, credible training and independent development institution.The Institute is renowned for providing academic leadership, training, research based evidence and critical analysis to support the formulation, implementation, monitoring and evaluation of policies.To accomplish its mission, the Institute serves as a platform which works in partnership with universities, governments, NGOs and a number of academic centers in the globe to conduct educational programmes, organize conferences, conduct researches and joint projects.
