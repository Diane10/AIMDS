BOARD OF DIRECTORS/GOVERNING COUNCIL

The Board of Director/Governing Council is the highest policy-making organ of the Institute and comprises the Chairperson, Vice Chairperson, Secretary and a number of Directors appointed by the Annual General Meeting(AGM) by virtue of the positions held, in a wide range of organizations representing the society. Its members representing a diversity of interests including the government, civil society, international agencies, private sector, development partners and academic institutions.The Board oversees the implementation of the programs and

activities of the institute, and monitors the utilization of resourcesThe Board is mandated to ensure that the organization operates within its mandate, preserves its values, and operates as a responsible corporate citizen.
