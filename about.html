About Us

Who We Are

African Institute of Management, Technology and Development Studies(AIMTDS) is a leading educational, research, credible training and independent development institution.The Institute is renowned for providing academic leadership, training, research based evidence and critical analysis to support the formulation, implementation, monitoring and evaluation of policies.

To accomplish its mission, the Institute serves as a platform which works in partnership with universities, governments, NGOs and a number of academic centers in the globe to conduct educational programmes, organize conferences, conduct researches and joint projects. AIMTDS provides academic and professional capacity building training, research,consultancy and advisory services.

Vision

Our vision is to be world-class centre of learning and research committed to critical and creative thinking skills and application of knowledge acquired to real life situations for humanity.

Mission

To be a leading institution with reputation for excellence in teaching and research and strong links with business, industry and government for knowledge, skills, character, freedom and sustainability.

Motto

The motto of the Institute is: Excellence, Service and Intergrity. Excellence and Integrity will be the watchwords in all our services.

Core Mandate

AIMTDS provides academic and professional capacity building training, research, consultancy and advisory services.

Location

The African Institute of Management, Technology and Development Studies is located within a serene environment at Kicukiro District in Kigali-Rwanda. The Institute is sited in a village called Umujyi wa Kigali. AIMTDS is less than 10 kilometers from the Kigali International Airport.

Institute Colours

The main colour of our Institute is GOLD, BLUE and WHITE. Gold symbolizes victory and success. Blue is a symbol of peace, justice, perseverance and vigilance. White symbolizes victory and success. All stakeholders are encouraged to work very hard to resonate with our colours.




History

Originally founded in 1996 as Development Africa Consortium, African Institute of Management, Technology and Development Studies(AIMTDS) is established by a group of African researchers and experts and joined by many partners worldwide. It has continued to play the role of a leading think tank in the Global South, and to serve as a reference point for relevant change processes.

Since its creation, AIMTDS managed to build a robust network involving various stakeholders such as researchers, experts, Ph.D Students, decision makers, practitioners, journalists, etc. from different spheres and scientific areas. Apart from running various academic and profesional programmes in collaboration with various academic institutions, the Institute has organized several international conferences and several training/building capacity workshops, provide expertise for many institutions, and published books and numerous papers which are globally distributed and recognized.

These events and publications cover many emerging research areas mainly related to the human-environment nexus from a multidimensional, multiscale, interdisciplinary, and policy-making perspectives. Through its initiatives, the AIMTDS attempts to provide expertise, to advance science and its applications, and to contribute to effective science and policy interactions.

Our programmes and services are organised in a high-quality academic environment that is client-focused and based on innovative approaches to learning and growing research culture. The Institute is non-sectarian in its programmes and committed to academic freedom and open inquiry.

Core Values

We cherish our six core values, which aim to build a reputation of excellence in learning, research and community services.The institution expects all staff, clients and other stakeholders to act in accordance with the values outlined below, and to assume personal and collective responsibility for assuring that these are embedded within our culture. These core values should be addressed in all circumstances and in all activities.

Logo

The logo of African Institute of Management, Technology and Development Studies consists of five stars, AIMTDS, a globe showing Africa and encircled symbolizing global scholarship. The general circle shape of the logo points to the global outlook of the Institute. In other words, our activities shall cut across race, gender, religious and political affiliations. The stars signify the fact that AIMTDS shall be a light unto humanity. The motto of the Institute is inscribed at the bottom of the logo.
