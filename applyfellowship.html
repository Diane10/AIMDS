African Fellowship Programme

Overview of African Fellowship Programme

African Fellowship Programme of the African Institute of Management, Technology and Development Studies is open to academics at other institutions wishing to contribute to academic activities at AIMTDS and partner institutions. This is an exciting opportunity for academics with passion for international exposure and significant international experience to contribute to building capacity in Africa.

The programme particularly provides opportunity for African scholars or scholars with an established or emerging international reputation to spend one to twelve months in the Institute. This is an opportunity to contribute to shaping our future and building a world class institution in Africa. A scholar whose application is successful will be granted affiliation as a Associate Fellow or Visiting Fellow or Visiting Senior Fellow during his/her duration of stay at the Institute. Application from scholars on sabbatical leave from home institution is highly welcome.

why African Fellowship Programme?

Fellowship Activities:

1.Engage in cutting-edge research, writing for journal publication/ grant writing, often on a joint project with one or more AIMTDS research staff.
2. Engage in teaching, training and consultancy at all levels using relevant methods, e.g. lectures, seminars, tutorials, etc, working as part of the academic team.
3. Contribute to supervision of students’ research project,
4. Facilitating/organising capacity-building/development workshops, etc.
5. Participate in curriculum development/course design and /or expand curriculum in emerging disciplines.
6. Fellows are expected to present at least one public lecture during their stay.
7. Participate in the Institute conferences
8. Fellows benefit from the educational resources and network of the Institute

Requirements

Applicants are expected to satisfy the following criteria when they submit their applications. The applicant must:

1. Hold a PhD or equivalent from a reputable university.A PhD student who has completed course, may be accepted as Associate Fellow
2. Be a member of a University or similar institution
3. Have the ability to fulfil the objectives proposed
4. Must have published academic articles that meet international standards.
5. Have proven ability in teaching and research

Programme Benefit

If the visiting Fellows have no other financial sponsorship, the Institute will provide transportation, health insurance and accommodation throughout the duration of their stay at the Institute or any other partner institution.

Application

An application should include the following documents:

• A Curriculum Vitae
• Data page of International Passport valid for at least one year
• A statement of the purpose/motivation of the visit
• The proposed relationship with on-going academic activity at AIMTDS
• A proposed work schedule
• A copy of at least three papers published in international journals.
• Two Letters of Reference

Deadline

Application deadlines are as follows: 31st of March and 30th of September each year. Applicants should note that it might take up to three months before fellowship starts.
